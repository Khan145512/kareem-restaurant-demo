Our Legacy

Kareem Restaurant

Since 1975, Kareem's has been serving the finest Mughlai and Indian cuisine, preserving age-old recipes passed down through generations. Our chefs bring the royal kitchens of Awadh to your plate.

We blend traditional spices with contemporary elegance to create an unforgettable dining experience. Every dish is a celebration of India's rich culinary heritage.

From our signature biryanis to silky dal makhani, each bite tells a story of passion and authenticity.
